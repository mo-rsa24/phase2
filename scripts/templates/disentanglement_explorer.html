What each experiment tests
  • Exp 1 — Baseline VAE (β=1). Are factors at all separated?
  • Exp 2 — β-VAE (β=4). Does stronger KL pressure improve disentanglement?
  • Exp 3 — Undercomplete (z=4). 4 dims for 5 factors — forced bottleneck.
  • Exp 4 — Overcomplete (z=20). Posterior collapse risk, redundancy.
  • Exps 20–23 — Supervised sweep. λ ∈ {1, 50, 1, 10} × βsup ∈ {1, 1, 0, 0}. 20=v1 diagnostic; 21=brute-force λ; 22=per-dim β alone; 23=both fixes (recommended).

Mutual Information Gap (MIG) ?
MIG ∈ [0, 1] — higher is more disentangled. Uses 3 000 random samples. Takes 30–120 s to compute. Click a factor row to see the conditional μ histogram for the dim that owns it.
